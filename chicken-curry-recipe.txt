1. Cut an onion and dice it.
2. Saute the diced onions in some canola oil.
3. Add salt to taste, three pods of green cardomom, and a pinch of turmeric powder.
4. After 10 min, add some Madras ship curry powder.
5. When the masala looks cooked after a while, add a pound of chicken.
6. Add some water, if you wish to have a thick gravy.
7. After about half an hour of cooking on stove on medium, check the chicken.
8. If chicken is cooked, then the dish is ready. 
9. Add some cilantro on top as garnish.
10. Dish is ready! Enjoy with rice, naan or any kind of flat bread!
